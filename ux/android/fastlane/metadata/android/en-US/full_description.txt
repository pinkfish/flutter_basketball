Stats for basketball.

Lets you breakdown a series of seasons/teams basketball stats by player and by game
to see trends on a per game basis as well as overall season trends for the team
along with trends on a per player basis.

Lets you control if you track where in the keyway the shots are taking from to give
an overview of where the hits come from and where the misses come from.

Lets you see a summary view of the team as well as per player summary views for
the game and for the season.  Creates customisable graphs to see which games players
had what sorts of results and how their games look over time.
